Improved Vanilla hud
By errur
======================================
Choose a folder depending on your aspect ratio.

IVHud_normal 	 	  - 4:3
IVHud_widescreen 	  - 16:9
(16:10 is untested, you can try 4:3 or 16:9 version but elements might overlaps)

Copy the folder into your custom folder located in (by default) C:/Program Files/Steam/steamapps/common/Team Fortress 2/tf/

For customization options check the customize folder.
